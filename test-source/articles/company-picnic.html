Test Security News announces annual company picnic and awards

Test Security News is delighted to announce its annual company picnic, taking place next month at Riverside Park. Employees and their families are invited for an afternoon of food, games, and the yearly staff awards ceremony.

This year's celebration marks the tenth anniversary of the publication, and the editorial team has prepared a retrospective of the newsroom's favourite stories from the past decade.

Attendees are encouraged to RSVP by the end of the week so catering can be finalized. Vegetarian and gluten-free options will be available, and the famous barbecue contest returns by popular demand.
